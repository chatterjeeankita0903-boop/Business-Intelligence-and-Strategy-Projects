## üè¶ Project 2: Bank Marketing Analytics Dashboard

### Overview
This project examines customer demographics and their relationship with **average account balance** to support data-driven marketing and segmentation strategies for banks.

## Key Highlights
- Married customers and tertiary-educated individuals maintain higher average account balances.
- Retired and management professionals contribute the highest account balances.
- Noticeable variation in average balances across education levels and occupations.
- Enables identification of high-value customer segments for targeted marketing campaigns.

### Dashboards & Insights
- Customer distribution by marital status, occupation, and education level  
- Analysis of average account balance across demographic segments  
- Identification of high-value customer profiles for targeted marketing  

### Outcome
The dashboard enables banks to design focused marketing campaigns and optimize customer acquisition and retention strategies using demographic insights.
