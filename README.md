## ğŸ“‰ Project 3: Bank Customer Churn & Behavioral Analytics Dashboard

### Overview
This project focuses on analyzing **customer churn behavior** in a retail banking context using Power BI. The dashboard combines customer demographics, financial attributes, product holdings, and engagement indicators to identify **key drivers of churn and high-risk customer segments**.

The objective is to enable banks to proactively design **data-driven retention strategies** by understanding who is churning, why they churn, and which customers are most valuable yet vulnerable.

### Key KPIs
- Total Customers  
- Active Customers  
- Churned Customers  
- Churn Rate (%)  
- Average Account Balance  
- Average Credit Score  

### Dashboards & Insights
- **Churn Analysis**
  - Churn rate across age brackets, gender, and geography
  - Higher churn observed among specific age groups and inactive customers

- **Product & Engagement Analysis**
  - Churn trends by number of products held
  - Impact of customer activity status on churn probability


### Analytical Features
- Age banding using calculated columns for demographic segmentation  
- KPI-driven executive summary using DAX measures  
- comparative visuals to highlight high-value churn-risk customers  

### Outcome
The dashboard delivers a **360-degree customer view**, helping banks:
- Identify churn-prone customer segments early
- Prioritize retention efforts for high-value customers
- Improve engagement strategies based on behavioral insights
- Support strategic decision-making with actionable analytics
